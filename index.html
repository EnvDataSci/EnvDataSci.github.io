Description

Environment Data Science is run by Mike Spencer. We specialise in solving your environmental data science problems using methods that are especially suited to awkward and large scale data. We present our results in visually engaging and informative ways. Please look through some examples of our services below to see what we can do. Get in touch to discuss your requirements, as a small company we are flexible and able to fit around your needs.

GIS

We've been working with Geographic Information Systems (GIS) and spatial data for over a decade. We've used all the big names: ArcGIS, MapInfo, even ERDAS Imagine. Our favourites are currently QGIS and GRASS. We use GIS for data visualisation, analysis and processing and fieldwork sampling design. We can advise you on where you can get data, for free or as a paid service. We can undertake GIS analysis for you, prepare data or end products (e.g. maps).

Data
visualisation

Let us produce beautiful summary plots of your data to give you a head start on your analysis. We use tools like R and QGIS to display our work and can prepare scripts to help you prepare plots with set formatting in the future. Please get in touch to discuss what you'd like to display and we can advise and script it for you.

Programming
and data wrangling

We specialise in working with awkward data, including: irregular time series (e.g. missing values), raster time series (e.g. satellite images), historic data, data from multiple sources, data spread across multiple files, data format conversion. You name it, we've probably banged our heads against a wall about it too.

We solve these data problems for you. We can write code to solve a particular task or develop a script to string together your workflow. This would be especially useful if you find yourself doing the same copying and pasting tasks each month. Your ready for analysis data can be returned to you in almost any format you require. We personally like SQLite databases as most software can interface with them and they're super quick.

Training

We offer bespoke training courses that include: Version control (e.g. GitHub); GIS: data collection, storage, analysis and presentation; R programming and data analysis. Our courses are taught by academics with experience in both the public and private sector.

These courses will take you from planning your data collection right through to producing your results focusing how to make each step repeatable. Our courses are modular, so you only need take the parts you require.

Innovatively, we can also teach on the job. For this we would base an instructor in your workplace for an agreed length of time to work alongside you, helping you develop your skills on problems that you face each day.
